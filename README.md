# 🛒 E-commerce Sales & Customer Insights Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to uncover key business insights related to revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional profitability.

The goal is to simulate a real-world data analyst workflow by performing **data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), SQL-based querying, and dashboard visualization**.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit trends over time
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Evaluate regional performance and profitability
- Detect loss-making products and areas for improvement

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

- **Python** (Pandas, Matplotlib) → Data cleaning & EDA
- **SQL** → Data querying and business analysis
- **Power BI** → Interactive dashboard & visualization
- **GitHub** → Version control and project documentation

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

### 📈 Sales Analysis

- Monthly and yearly sales trends
- Category-wise and product-wise performance

### 👥 Customer Analysis

- Top customers by revenue
- Customer contribution to total sales

### 🌍 Regional Analysis

- Sales and profit by region
- Identification of high-performing and underperforming regions

### 💰 Profitability Analysis

- Profit vs Sales relationship
- Identification of loss-making products

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features (Power BI)

- KPI cards (Total Sales, Profit, Orders)
- Sales trend over time
- Region-wise and category-wise breakdown
- Interactive filters (Region, Category, Date)

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- A small percentage of customers contribute to a large portion of revenue
- Certain regions generate high sales but lower profits, indicating discount or cost issues
- Technology category shows strong profitability compared to others
- Some products consistently generate losses and need pricing or supply chain review
